Event Planning

Mannheim is a Unesco City of Music. We at LuvMusic are intimately connected with the music scene here in Mannheim and the surrounding area. These connections allow us to organize exciting events, from live shows to jam sessions to local nights out. If you are an aspiring musician, a seasoned professional or simply looking for a good time, our network of producers, promoters, venue owners and eager audiences can offer something for anyone interested in deepening their musical experience.

Jam Database

We safely and securely maintain a database of artists and producers that are ready and willing to colaborate. Are you having trouble finding a violinist or keyboardist to complete your band? Are you looking for a technician to mix and master your project? Maybe a professional to shoot and edit your next viral video? Join our community and take advantage of the available opportunities.
